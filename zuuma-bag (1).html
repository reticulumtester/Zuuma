We come from the film industry, where our mentors always taught us that design should serve function — a good idea is recognisable by one simple thing: it works.

"Would I carry this bag myself — with maximum standards for function and design?"

The Zuuma Bag was born from a real frustration. After many tests, we found the balance. One bag for all of it. Minimal by design. Built to last. We speak to everyone and exclude no one.
